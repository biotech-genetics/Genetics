About

Find Out More About Us

It's a website created entirely by the passionate students from the department of Genetics, faculty of Agriculture, Alexandria university.

This website aims to showcase the graduation projects undertaken by the students of the departments in year 2024

The topics for those projects are variable & they tackle many different subjects dealing with a heafty amount of organisms to discuss their biotechnological potential!

  • Statement of purpose

    We believe that learning by doing is the way to go when it comes laboratory experiments & scientific research, & those two aspects are the backbone for the work nature at our department

We had worked with a group of amazing doctors & staff members

they have guided us with patience & care all throughout the project duration, providing us with valuable information
