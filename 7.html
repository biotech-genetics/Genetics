(2)Drosophila hadi(patternson 8 whalter) Diptera, family Drosophilidae, genus Drosophila, species hadi,male have 5 chromosomes, female have 6 chromosomes.In addition to Drosophila melanogaster order Diptera, family Drosophilidae, genus Drosophila, species melanogaster, Which is mainly found in that region.
